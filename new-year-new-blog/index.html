03 January 2015 on update

A Writer's Resolution

As part of my New Year's resolutions, I've resolved to write more often. I've gotten back in the spirit of writing as a continuation of the prepping for the GRE with issue and argument essays. So I've setup this blog to consolidate personal thoughts and technical details about recent and past projects.

Types of Posts

To focus in on the types of posts that I will feature in this blog, I will hold myself accountable to posts with the following tags:

  • update: Any week-to-week updates regarding recent events or happenings

  • tech: Technical details of any projects I've been working on or anything technically interesting

  • interesting: Remarks I have on interesting things from time to time
