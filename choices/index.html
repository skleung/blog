Choices

27 February 2015 on DYL

Doors.
Pathways to walk through
convey a new place where I choose
To learn.
and push intellectual boundaries
or wonder if it's my destiny
To take time off.
Where the allure of the valley
may possibly draw me
To wonder.
how I know what God's will is
not to be passive but active
To trust.
that no door is the true way
and that my faith in the future say
To choose.
into the Kingdom
and stop running away from
Choices.

Sherman Leung

Student, Teacher, Developer, Designer.

Stanford, CA http://shermanleung.com
