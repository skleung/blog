CS198 [Winter 2015 ed.]

15 January 2015

This quarter, I'm working as a small group leader and a section leader for CS106B. And it's crazy to look back and see how much I've learned about teaching over the past 3 quarters. As I've been encouraged to perform some introspection on my teaching style as a small group leader, I've realized how the various styles of teaching that I've taken from teachers throughout my student life has blended in this unique blend of teaching that I've come to recognize and appreciate.

I'd say CS198 Small Group Leading is definitely a fantastic way to get involved with the CS198 community, and I'm looking forward to the quarter with a new group of SL's (groupees) and a brand new group of sectionees for CS106B. Here's to a new quarter of teaching!

Sherman Leung

Student, Teacher, Developer, Designer.
