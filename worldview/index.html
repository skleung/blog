Worldview

06 February 2015 on DYL

We were asked this week to consider our personal worldviews as a part of Dave Evans + Bill Burnett's Designing Your Life course.

As a Christian, a lot of my decisions and perspectives are rooted in my faith and how it manifests itself in my life. Discovering what that means and how to do it is very much a process of discernment and co-creation with God to discover what His will is. My perspective on what's good, righteous, and just is based on my interpretation of who God is.

Separate (but also tied to in a sense) from my faith, I believe that belief in anything shouldn't just a cognitive conciousness but an intentional and full-bodied manifestation into who you are no matter what you believe, and I try to reflect that view in my own life to the best of my ability.

Sherman Leung

Student, Teacher, Developer, Designer.

Stanford, CA http://shermanleung.com
